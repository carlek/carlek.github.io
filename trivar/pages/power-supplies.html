Electronic Ballasts

High-frequency electronic ballasts for fluorescent and specialty lamp applications.

Overview

Built for Demanding
Lighting Applications

TriVar's high-frequency electronic ballasts are designed for fluorescent and specialty lamp applications across a wide range of industries. Operating at high frequency, TriVar ballasts eliminate flicker, improve lamp efficacy, and reduce energy consumption compared to conventional magnetic ballasts.

Available in AC and DC input configurations, TriVar ballasts are engineered for use in energy-efficient, industrial, outdoor, and specialty applications including UV disinfection, germicidal treatment, photochemical processing, and locomotive environments.

Explosion-proof and battery-operated portable luminaire versions are also available for hazardous location and field use requirements.
