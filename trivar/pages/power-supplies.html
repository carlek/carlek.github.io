Power Supplies & Inverters

Custom-designed industrial pure sine wave power supplies and inverters for critical applications.

Overview

Uninterrupted, Transient-Free
Sine Wave Power

TriVar manufactures custom-designed 50/60Hz industrial pure sine wave power supplies and inverters. Each unit delivers uninterrupted, transient-free, current-limited sine wave output — engineered for demanding manufacturing, process-control, and communications environments.

Systems are available across a wide voltage and power range, with single-phase and three-phase input and output configurations. TriVar also produces frequency converters, THD correcting modules, SMPS units, and high-frequency GFI modules.

All units are custom-designed to specification — contact TriVar with your voltage, frequency, power, and application requirements.
