Product Line 03

Motor Speed Controls

Solid state variable speed controls for AC motors — UL Listed and CSA Certified.

Overview

Precision Speed Regulation
for AC Motors

TriVar solid state motor speed controls provide reliable variable speed regulation for shaded-pole, permanent split capacitor, and universal AC motors. Rated from under 1 amp to 20 amps at 120V/60Hz or 240V/50Hz, each control is individually tested with nine automatic tests before shipment.

All TriVar controls include a built-in RFI filter and are factory-calibrated for low trim settings. Self-extinguishing flame-retardant ABS plastic enclosures are standard; custom aluminum enclosures are available on request.

Optional features include dual trim sets, voltage compensation, and custom leads. Available in skeleton mount, cabinet mount, and wall mount configurations to suit any installation.
