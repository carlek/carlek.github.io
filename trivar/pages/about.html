Company Overview

Who We Are

TriVar Inc. was established in 1980 as a developer and manufacturer of proprietary solid state power electronic control technology. For over four decades the company has served Original Equipment Manufacturers across industrial, communications, and process control sectors.

All product development and manufacturing takes place at TriVar's facility in Brampton, Ontario, Canada. The company maintains an active R&D program and holds patents on its core technology.

TriVar products are listed as applicable by UL, CSA, and World Standards Associations, meeting the compliance requirements for customers in North America and global export markets.
