Touch Lamp Controls

Solid state touch-sensitive control modules for lamp manufacturers and OEM applications.

Overview

Touch Control Technology
for Lamp Manufacturers

TriVar touch lamp controls are solid state OEM modules that enable touch-sensitive switching and dimming in table lamps, floor lamps, and bedside fixtures. A single touch to any conductive part of the lamp body cycles through brightness levels or toggles power — no mechanical switch required.

Designed for integration into lamp assemblies at the manufacturing stage, TriVar modules are compact, reliable, and engineered for long service life. Custom configurations are available to match specific fixture designs and wiring requirements.

Contact TriVar to discuss your fixture type, lamp load, and production requirements — we will specify the right module for your application.
