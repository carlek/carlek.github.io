ConcertLight II

Cordless rechargeable music stand light — distributed in Canada by TriVar.

Overview

Ample, Even Light
Wherever You Perform

The ConcertLight II is a cordless, rechargeable music stand light manufactured by LampCraft and distributed in Canada by TriVar. Designed for musicians who rehearse and perform in low-light venues where power access is limited, it delivers ample, even illumination without glare or hot-spots — reducing eyestrain during long sessions.

The daylight-spectrum lamp is rated for 30,000 hours of operation. A spring-clip design makes it compatible with most music stands, including folding models. An optional piano clip secures the light to grand pianos and furniture stands.

Runs on rechargeable NiMH battery or AC power. A 230VAC international adapter is available for touring musicians.
